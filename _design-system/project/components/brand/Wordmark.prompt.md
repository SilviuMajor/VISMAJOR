The two house marks. `brand="gyno"` is the loud product wordmark (heavy, tight); `brand="vismajor"` is the refined house mark (light, widely tracked).

```jsx
<Wordmark brand="gyno" size={64} descriptor="Nipple Tightening Cream" />
<Wordmark brand="vismajor" size={30} align="center" />
```

Use GY-NO! on product/marketing; VIS MAJOR as the house signature (footers, back-of-pack, about).
